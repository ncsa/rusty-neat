GRCh38 region-sharded; the lever is exclusive-node availability, not packing

Threading is break-even at best on Delta's dual-socket EPYC; processes scale ~3.5× to a bandwidth plateau. So whole-genome runs shard into single-thread region-workers across exclusive nodes: a naive shared-partition run hit 3 h 41 m (cross-tenant contention), exclusive K=2 packing 2 h 30 m, and the compute floor is ~30 min when enough whole nodes are available. Source: ACCESS report §3.6.
