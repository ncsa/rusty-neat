Somatic recall vs tumor purity

Mutect2 recall on rneat tumor/normal pairs (chr22), sweeping purity at a fixed total sequencing budget split between tumor and matched normal.

Recall is flat (0.91–0.94) from 0.3 to 0.7 purity, then collapses to 0.17 at 0.9 — not an rneat artifact but an experiment-design one. Under a fixed budget, purity 0.9 starves the matched normal to ~6×, so Mutect2 loses the germline reference it needs to subtract. Recommendation: hold normal coverage fixed (don't split a fixed budget) when sweeping purity. Source: ACCESS report §3.9.
